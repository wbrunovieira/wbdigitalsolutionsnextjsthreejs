## 1. Executive summary

- The site is **effectively invisible in organic search outside its own brand.**
- 90-day totals: **912 impressions, 19 clicks, avg position 28.1, CTR 2.08%.**
- ~74% of impressions are **branded** ("wb digital", "wb automation", "wb ai",
  plus misspellings). Brand terms rank fine (pos 4–8); everything else does not.
- The only non-brand theme with real demand is **emotional (web) design** — 184+
  impressions on `emotional website design` alone, but buried at **position 82**.
  The blog post `/blog/how-emotional-design-can` is the single page attracting
  non-brand interest, sitting at position 69.
- Money pages (`/websites`, `/automation`, `/ai`) rank well (pos 6–8) but draw
  almost no impressions → they are **not targeting/ranking for real
  service-intent keywords**.
- For a 2-year-old site this points to **weak topical authority + thin non-brand
  content**, not indexation newness.

## 4. Interpretation (for a 2-year-old site)

1. **Brand-only visibility.** After 2 years, non-brand demand capture is near
   zero (235 impressions/90d). Root cause is topical authority + thin non-brand
   content, not crawl/index newness (technical baseline is 100/100).
2. **One proven non-brand theme exists: emotional design.** Google already
   associates the domain with it (184+ impressions) but the ranking page sits on
   page 6–9. This is the shortest path to a page-1 non-brand foothold.
3. **Service/commercial keywords are unclaimed.** The pages that should win
   clients (websites/systems/automation/ai) rank when shown but are shown for
   almost nothing → they don't target the queries buyers actually use.
